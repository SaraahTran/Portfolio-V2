Overview

4 friends and I from University decided to work in a team on a project together. We all wanted to contribute to this project as we all had different skills. We wanted to make a web app using electron.js. The web app will have a variety of features for students including notes, calendar, flashcards and a chat. My role was the UX/UI designer.

Design

I generally prefer designing lighter themes but a few of my team members requested a darker theme. So I came up with the idea of having a light mode and dark mode to satisfy everyone.

My first task was to design the homepage. I placed the main menu bar at the top and in the middle, I have a to do list and calendar. Underneath is all the features of the app which include notes, calendar, flashcards and a chat. I believe that it is important to have a simple user interface and for it to be colourful, as it makes the design more enticing.
