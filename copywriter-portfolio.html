Website

Alex Ruel works as a copywriter and he asked me to build him a portfolio website that would showcase his work and skills.

Design

Because this is a copywriter portfolio, I wanted to design the website with a writing and typewriting kind of aesthetic.

I decided to make the background in the hero section writing paper. For the images, I used free stock images and placed them in polaroids.

Tools

I decided to use Wordpress with a site builder called Oxygen. I really like this plugin as it is fast and made for developers.

Key Takeaways

The client requested for me to put placeholder text as it is technically his job to do all the writing. It also made me realise that I should learn basic SEO techniques and some website copywriting. There are a lot of parts to a website and I should attempt to at least know a little bit of everything.
